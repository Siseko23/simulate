Why does 20ft carry more than 40ft on SA roads?
Under the National Road Traffic Act (NRTA) and SANS 1518, a 20ft container sits on a shorter skeletal chassis - its weight is concentrated over fewer axles, allowing a higher payload per unit within the legal gross vehicle mass. A 40ft container spreads the same GVM over a longer chassis, reducing the available payload. This is the opposite of what most shippers expect and is unique to SA road legislation.
