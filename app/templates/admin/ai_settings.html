How the AI engine works

  1. Filter suppliers by route match and vehicle availability
  2. Exclude suppliers outside the proximity radius
  3. Calculate composite score for each remaining supplier
  4. Auto-select the highest-scoring supplier. If tied, price is tiebreaker
  5. Notify supplier. If rejected/timeout → auto-reassign to next
