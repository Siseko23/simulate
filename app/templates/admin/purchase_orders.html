Payment rule

Suppliers are always paid their quoted rate - the supplierRate field on each PO - not the shipper-facing price. The platform fee is the difference between agreedRate (what the shipper paid) and supplierRate (what the supplier quoted). This means FreightFlow retains its fee regardless of the shipper price negotiated. A payment can only be released after an invoice is uploaded and verified by the Finance team.
