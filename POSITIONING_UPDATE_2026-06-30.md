## Product positioning principle

FFN should not claim to out-scale established enterprise freight networks early on. Its edge is:

- faster onboarding,
- visible pricing,
- SME friendliness,
- owner-driver access,
- integrated driver workflow,
- QR POD and label proof,
- FreightFlow AI/Kargo decision support.
